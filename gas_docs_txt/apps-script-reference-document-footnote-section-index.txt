FootnoteSection elements represent the text for a Footnote.
FootnoteSection can contain ListItem or Paragraph elements.
FootnoteSection provides methods to manipulate its contents, such as appending, inserting, clearing, and removing Paragraphs.
You can search for elements or text patterns within a FootnoteSection.
Methods are available to get and set attributes, text content, and text alignment of a FootnoteSection.
