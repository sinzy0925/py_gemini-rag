Visibility
An enum that represents the visibility state of the widgets.
Visibility.VISIBLE
means that the widget is being displayed and is visible.
Visibility.HIDDEN
means that the widget is not displayed and is invisible.
To call an enum, call its parent class, name, and property. For example,
CardService.Visibility.VISIBLE
.
Only available through the
Gemini Alpha
program
for Google Workspace add-ons that extend Google Workspace Flows.
