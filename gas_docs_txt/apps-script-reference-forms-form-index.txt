The document details numerous methods for the

The document also lists methods for managing form content structure, permissions, and includes notes on deprecated methods.
Form

responses are stored. Items include question items like checkboxes or radio items, while layout
items refer to things like page breaks. Forms can be accessed or created from
Form
App
